school library

Was Established With The School

The school library is a place where you can come with your class. Then you can borrow up to 2 books and read them for a week. After that you can return it and get a new one.

school cafeteria

Was Built With The School

The school cafeteria was a place where you could come to get school lunch before covid. Before covid if it rained during lunch time then we would go inside the cafeteria and eat. We would also go there for assemblies.
